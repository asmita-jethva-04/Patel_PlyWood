Our Story

Crafting Premium Interior Solutions Since 2010

Patel Plywood Centre was founded in 2010 with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide the finest quality plywood, veneers, laminates, and interior solutions to architects, designers, and homeowners who refuse to compromise on quality.

Over the past 14+ years, we have grown from a small local supplier to a trusted name in the interior industry, serving over 850+ happy clients across India. Our commitment to excellence, genuine branded materials, and expert guidance has made us the preferred choice for residential and commercial projects.

Our Journey

The Patel Plywood Story

2010 Founded
Humble Beginnings

Patel Plywood Centre was established in Ahmedabad with a small showroom and a big dream to revolutionize the interior industry with premium quality products.

2014 Growth
Expansion & Recognition

Expanded our product range to include laminates, veneers, and decorative panels. Gained recognition as a trusted supplier for architects and interior designers.

2018 Innovation
Innovation & Diversification

Introduced cutting-edge products including WPC, MDF, CNC design panels, and sustainable flooring solutions. Became a one-stop destination for all interior needs.

2024 Today
Leading Interior Solutions Provider

Today, Patel Plywood Centre serves over 850+ satisfied clients, offers 21+ product categories, and continues to set new standards of excellence in the interior industry.
